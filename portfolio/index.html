Bio

I am a self-taught sysadmin, fullstack ruby, soon-to-be fullstack javascript developer and Game Developer. A high-school dropout; I couldn't take the slow, grueling pace of the organized curriculum. Inspired by great things accomplished by small teams and my natural inclination toward technology I bought a few C++ and Visual Basic 6 books and set about the mad science of software and game development. After a year I felt confident enough to try my hand at game development and made a few small role-playing games in Visual Basic then moved on to DirectX 8 and C++ to create a few Isometric action-adventure games. Unsatisfied with my skill-set I got myself a VPS and through trial and error learned how to administer the unwieldy beast to build stacks and host some private servers just to fool around with.

This trend continues as I am still spending my time expanding my skill-set and learning anything interesting or new that crosses my path. Currently I am studying full-stack Javasscript with Node and MongoDB while honing my front-end skills through Free Code Camp and the power of Google.
